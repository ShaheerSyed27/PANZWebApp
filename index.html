About Us

Our Story & Mission

ہماری کہانی اور مشن

Since 1989 - Building Community

The Pakistan Association of New Zealand (PANZ) was established in 1989 by a group of dedicated Pakistani immigrants who recognised the need for a unified voice and support system for the growing Pakistani community in Aotearoa.

Over three decades, PANZ has grown from a small gathering of families to a nationwide organisation serving thousands of Pakistani Kiwis across Auckland, Wellington, Christchurch, and beyond.

We are proudly non-political, non-religious, and non-profit - focusing solely on bringing our community together, preserving our rich cultural heritage, and helping new migrants integrate successfully into New Zealand society.

🎯 Our Core Values

  • 1
    Unity & Inclusion Embracing all Pakistanis regardless of province, language, or background
  • 2
    Cultural Pride Celebrating and preserving Pakistani heritage for future generations
  • 3
    Integration Support Helping members thrive in New Zealand while staying connected to roots
  • 4
    Community Service Giving back through volunteering, mentorship, and charitable work
